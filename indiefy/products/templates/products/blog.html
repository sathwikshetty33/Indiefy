Corona changed our course and life..

Meena kumari, a local painter near kadirenahalli cross,Bengaluru, says that the times after covid pandemic are not the same as before. The sales remain seasonal, attracting many mostly during any festivals. The price they quote would be forsay Rs.1000, but people bargain and end up paying Rs.500-600 only. She wishes to expand their business and has the liking to sell her products online but has never been approached by any group or supporters. She feels any person like her must be given the opportunity to show their work and talent which deserves a right price.

We had to move from Gujarat to gain profits..

Lata , a local painter near kadirenahalli cross , Bengaluru, shares with us the journey of her shop. She was enthusiastic to say that they are from a village in Gujarat which is 10km from that of our prime minister Narendra Modi ji. The whole family had to move to bengaluru as the product prize range was comparitivelly less back there and earning a livelihood through their work was not compensating with their expenditure on making it..
